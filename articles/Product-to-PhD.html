Product to PhD: A New Policy Catchphrase or a Structural Shift?

Moving Beyond Slogans Toward Purpose-Driven Academic Reform

✍️ Dr. Anand Pal 📅 February 13, 2026 ⏱️ 8 min read

“Product to PhD” appears to be emerging as a new catchphrase for academic policymakers. However, without meaningful structural reform and real intellectual depth, it risks becoming another passing trend, similar to the earlier overemphasis on “publications” and the more recent fixation on “patents.”

If this idea is to carry real value, it must be anchored in a clear purpose and systemic support. India should articulate its own objectives in line with its long-term national vision and societal needs, rather than relying on externally defined benchmarks or adopting fashionable terminology without context.
