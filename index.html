About Me

I am an Associate Professor of Physics with a strong interest in materials science, emerging technologies, and global higher education. My work combines research, teaching, and academic leadership, with a focus on building scientific capacity and international collaborations.

I believe that science education must not only transfer knowledge but also shape intellectual character. My approach integrates conceptual clarity, historical context, and critical thinking to help students understand both the power and responsibility of scientific progress.

Research Interests

  • Advanced Functional Materials
  • Energy Storage Systems
  • Quantum Technologies
  • Science & Technology Policy
